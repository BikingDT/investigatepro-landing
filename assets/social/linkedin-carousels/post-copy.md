## Carousel 2: PEEPO Framework

**File:** `peepo-framework-carousel.pdf`

### Post Caption (Option A - Educational)

Most investigators focus on what went wrong.

The PEEPO framework asks a better question: What ALLOWED it to go wrong?

🔵 P — People (human factors)
🔵 E — Environment (physical conditions)  
🔵 E — Equipment (tools and machinery)
🔵 P — Procedures (the documented way)
🔵 O — Organization (where root causes live)

Swipe through to learn what to look for in each category ➡️

The key insight: Every People, Environment, Equipment, and Procedure factor traces back to an ORGANIZATIONAL decision.

That's where your root causes are.
That's where you can actually make change.

Save this for your next investigation. 🔖

### Post Caption (Option B - Contrarian hook)

"The worker made a mistake."

That's not a root cause. That's where most investigations STOP.

The PEEPO framework ensures you go deeper:

P — People → What human factors contributed?
E — Environment → What conditions set the stage?
E — Equipment → What tools/machinery failed?
P — Procedures → Were they practical to follow?
O — Organization → What system allowed this?

Swipe through for the complete breakdown ➡️

Here's the truth: if your root cause is "human error," you haven't finished investigating.

Ask WHY the error was possible.

That's where you'll find something you can actually fix.
