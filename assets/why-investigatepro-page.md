## Section 1: The Problem

### The Uncomfortable Truth About Incident Investigations

Most investigations don't prevent incidents. They document them.

**Here's what we see across industries:**
- 60% of serious incidents are variations of previous ones
- 85% of corrective actions are "retrain workers"
- Average investigation takes 2-3 weeks
- Leadership gets reports, not insights

**Why?** Because most investigations stop at "human error."

Human error isn't a root cause. It's where lazy investigations STOP.

The real root causes are organizational:
- Budget decisions
- Staffing decisions
- Procedure design
- Equipment procurement
- Cultural factors

Those are what InvestigatePro helps you find.

## Section 2: The Solution

### What Makes InvestigatePro Different

**1. ICAM Methodology Built In**

Not just forms to fill out. Real investigation guidance.

Every investigation follows the ICAM framework:
- Structured data collection
- PEEPO analysis (People, Environment, Equipment, Procedures, Organization)
- Systematic root cause identification
- Hierarchy of Controls for corrective actions

You can't skip steps. You can't stop at "human error."

**2. AI Investigation Coaching**

Like having an expert looking over your shoulder.

The AI:
- Suggests questions you might not think to ask
- Challenges weak findings
- Identifies similar past incidents
- Flags ineffective corrective actions

Any investigator can reach organizational root causes — not just experts.

**3. Pattern Recognition Across Incidents**

Stop investigating in isolation.

InvestigatePro connects the dots:
- "This incident is similar to 3 others in the past 12 months"
- "These 5 incidents share the same organizational factor"
- "This corrective action type has a 70% failure rate"

Organizational learning, not organizational amnesia.

**4. Corrective Action That Actually Happens**

Findings without action are just paperwork.

- Automatic assignment and tracking
- Hierarchy of Controls scoring (push for effective controls)
- Escalation when actions are overdue
- Verification before close-out

No more corrective actions disappearing into filing cabinets.
