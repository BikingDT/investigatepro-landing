# 60-Second Explainer Video Script

**Format:** Animated explainer / screen recording hybrid
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Purpose:** Homepage hero, social ads, LinkedIn

---

## Script

**[0:00-0:05] HOOK**

*Visual: Incident report being filed, then same incident happening again*

**NARRATOR:**
"The same incident. Again. Sound familiar?"

---

**[0:05-0:15] PROBLEM**

*Visual: Investigation form with "human error" circled, frustrated safety manager*

**NARRATOR:**
"Most incident investigations stop at 'human error.' They recommend more training. And six months later... the same incident happens again."

---

**[0:15-0:25] WHY**

*Visual: Iceberg graphic — "human error" above water, organizational factors below*

**NARRATOR:**
"Because human error isn't the root cause. It's where lazy investigations STOP. The real root causes are organizational — decisions about budgets, staffing, and systems."

---

**[0:25-0:40] SOLUTION**

*Visual: InvestigatePro interface, PEEPO analysis, AI coaching prompts*

**NARRATOR:**
"InvestigatePro is different. Built on ICAM methodology with AI coaching, we guide your team from surface symptoms to organizational root causes. Every time."

---

**[0:40-0:50] RESULTS**

*Visual: Stats appearing on screen*

**NARRATOR:**
"The result? Sixty percent faster investigations. Forty percent fewer repeat incidents. Root causes you can actually fix."

---

**[0:50-0:60] CTA**

*Visual: InvestigatePro logo, "Start Free Trial" button*

**NARRATOR:**
"Stop investigating the same incidents over and over. Start your free trial at investigatepro.app."

---

## Production Notes

**Tone:** Professional but accessible. Not corporate stiff.

**Visuals:**
- Clean, modern animation style
- Navy (#0C142E) and Cyan (#76DCEC) brand colors
- Mix of conceptual animation and product UI
- Stats should animate in with impact

**Music:** Upbeat but not cheesy. Think "confident problem-solver"

**Voiceover:** Male or female, warm but authoritative. Think NPR meets TED talk.

---

## Variations

**LinkedIn Ad (30 sec):** Cut to 0:00-0:15 + 0:40-0:60
**Instagram/TikTok (15 sec):** Hook + CTA only
**YouTube Pre-roll (6 sec):** "Stop investigating the same incidents. InvestigatePro."
