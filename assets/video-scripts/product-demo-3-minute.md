# 3-Minute Product Demo Video Script

**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover
**Duration:** 3 minutes
**Purpose:** Website demo page, sales follow-up, YouTube

---

## Script

**[0:00-0:15] INTRO**

*Visual: InvestigatePro dashboard*

**NARRATOR:**
"Let me show you how InvestigatePro transforms incident investigations. In the next three minutes, you'll see how we take you from incident to insight — with real root causes, not just 'human error.'"

---

**[0:15-0:45] INCIDENT CREATION**

*Visual: Creating new incident, mobile app view*

**NARRATOR:**
"When an incident happens, any team member can start the investigation immediately. Our mobile app works on-site — even offline.

Capture photos, initial statements, and basic details right where the incident occurred. Evidence is fresh, memories are clear.

Everything syncs automatically when you're back online."

---

**[0:45-1:30] PEEPO ANALYSIS**

*Visual: Walking through PEEPO categories, AI prompts appearing*

**NARRATOR:**
"Here's where InvestigatePro is different. Instead of a blank form, we guide investigators through the PEEPO framework.

People factors — fatigue, training, experience.
Environment — conditions, layout, housekeeping.
Equipment — tools, machinery, maintenance state.
Procedures — what should happen versus what actually happens.

Watch what happens when I enter a finding..."

*Visual: AI coaching prompt appears*

**NARRATOR:**
"The AI coaching kicks in. It's asking questions I might not have thought of. 'What organizational factors created this time pressure?' 'Has this equipment been involved in previous incidents?'

This is how we ensure every investigator — regardless of experience — reaches organizational root causes."

---

**[1:30-2:00] ORGANIZATIONAL FACTORS**

*Visual: Tracing finding to organizational factor*

**NARRATOR:**
"Now watch the key step. Every surface finding gets traced back to an organizational factor.

Worker rushed? That traces to time pressure. Which traces to staffing decisions. Which traces to a budget decision.

THAT'S your root cause. THAT'S what you can actually change.

InvestigatePro won't let you stop at 'human error.' The system ensures you dig deeper."

---

**[2:00-2:30] CORRECTIVE ACTIONS**

*Visual: Assigning corrective actions, Hierarchy of Controls*

**NARRATOR:**
"Once we identify root causes, we assign corrective actions. But not just any actions.

See this Hierarchy of Controls guidance? The system pushes you toward engineering controls — solutions that work even when humans make mistakes.

'Retrain workers' is a last resort, not a default.

Every action gets assigned to an owner, given a due date, and tracked to completion. No more corrective actions disappearing into filing cabinets."

---

**[2:30-2:50] DASHBOARD & PATTERNS**

*Visual: Leadership dashboard, pattern recognition*

**NARRATOR:**
"Leadership gets real-time visibility. Open investigations, overdue actions, trending factors.

And here's the real power — pattern recognition across investigations. 'These five incidents share a common organizational factor.' One insight that prevents incidents across your entire operation."

---

**[2:50-3:00] CTA**

*Visual: InvestigatePro logo, free trial offer*

**NARRATOR:**
"That's InvestigatePro. ICAM methodology. AI coaching. Real root causes.

Start your free trial at investigatepro.app. No credit card required."

---

## Production Notes

**Visuals:**
- Clean screen recording (1080p or 4K)
- Cursor highlights / zoom on key features
- Use demo environment with realistic data
- Subtle transitions between sections

**Voiceover:** Same voice as 60-second video for consistency

**Captions:** Include for accessibility and sound-off viewing

**Music:** Very subtle background, don't compete with narration
