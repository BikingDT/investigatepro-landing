# Customer Testimonial Video Template

**Format:** Interview-style testimonial
**Duration:** 90-120 seconds
**Purpose:** Social proof, website, sales materials

---

## Interview Questions

Use these questions to guide the conversation. Edit responses into a cohesive story.

### The Before (30 sec)

**Q1:** "Before InvestigatePro, what was your investigation process like?"
*Looking for: Pain points, frustrations, time spent, quality issues*

**Q2:** "What was the biggest challenge you faced with incident investigations?"
*Looking for: Specific pain point that resonates with prospects*

**Q3:** "Were you seeing the same types of incidents recurring?"
*Looking for: Repeat incident problem, frustration*

### The Change (30 sec)

**Q4:** "What made you decide to try InvestigatePro?"
*Looking for: Trigger event, what they were looking for*

**Q5:** "What was the implementation process like?"
*Looking for: Easy, fast, supportive (overcome objections)*

**Q6:** "How did your team respond to the new system?"
*Looking for: Adoption, ease of use, any initial concerns overcome*

### The After (30-60 sec)

**Q7:** "What results have you seen since implementing InvestigatePro?"
*Looking for: Specific metrics, time saved, repeat incidents reduced*

**Q8:** "Can you share a specific investigation that went differently because of InvestigatePro?"
*Looking for: Story, specific example, organizational factor found*

**Q9:** "What would you say to someone considering InvestigatePro?"
*Looking for: Recommendation, key benefit in their words*

## Production Notes

**Setup:**
- Professional but not sterile setting (office, control room, site)
- Good lighting (natural or professional kit)
- Lapel mic for clean audio
- Shoot in 4K, deliver in 1080p

**Interview Tips:**
- Let them talk naturally, edit later
- Get them to rephrase questions in answers
- Ask for specific numbers/examples
- Silence is okay — people fill it with good content

**B-Roll to Capture:**
- Them using InvestigatePro on computer/tablet
- Walking through facility
- Safety-related activities (appropriate to show)
- Team meetings, dashboards

**Lower Thirds:**
- Name
- Title
- Company (if they approve)
- Industry (if company anonymous)
