### Weekly Metrics

- Conversions: Trial signups, demo bookings

- Leads generated (by source)
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate

- Content piece performance ranking
- Cost per lead (if running ads)

- Demo bookings: 30
- Paid conversions: 5-10

- Full case study (blog/PDF)
- Summary post (LinkedIn)
- Stats graphic (social)
- Email reference
- Sales deck inclusion

- Recording (gated download)
- Key clips (social)

## Notes

- Adjust based on performance (double down on what works)
- Engage with comments within 1 hour of posting
- LinkedIn algorithm favors native content (no links in post body)
- Tuesday-Thursday are highest engagement days
- Morning posts (8-10 AM target timezone) perform best
