## SafetyCulture (iAuditor)

### Overview
- **What they are:** Inspection & checklist platform that added incident reporting
- **Pricing:** $24-$49/user/month (scales quickly with team size)
- **Strength:** Broad platform, strong mobile app, large customer base
- **Weakness:** Jack of all trades, master of none. Incident module is bolted-on.

### When They Come Up
**Prospect says:** "We're looking at SafetyCulture too."

**Response:**
"SafetyCulture is great for inspections and checklists. We see a lot of companies using them for that.

But here's the thing — their incident investigation module was added later. It's not built around any investigation methodology.

InvestigatePro is ICAM-native. Every investigation follows a systematic approach to reach organizational root causes. SafetyCulture lets you record what happened. We help you understand WHY it happened.

If inspections are your primary need, SafetyCulture might be right. If preventing repeat incidents is the goal, that's what we're built for."

### Key Differentiators
| Feature | InvestigatePro | SafetyCulture |
|---------|----------------|---------------|
| Built-in methodology (ICAM) | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| AI investigation coaching | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Organizational factor tracking | ✅ Native | ⚠️ Manual |
| Hierarchy of Controls scoring | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Cross-incident pattern recognition | ✅ Yes | ⚠️ Limited |
| Price for 10 users | $149/mo | $240-490/mo |

### Handle the Objection
**"SafetyCulture has more features."**

"You're right — they do more things. But we do ONE thing really well: incident investigation.

The question is whether you need a Swiss Army knife or a scalpel. If your investigations aren't preventing repeat incidents, you need depth, not breadth."
