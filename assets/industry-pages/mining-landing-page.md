### ICAM Methodology Built for Your Reality

**1. Mobile-First Data Collection**
Start investigations at the face, in the pit, at the plant. Offline capability means no waiting for connectivity. Photos, statements, timeline — captured immediately.

**2. PEEPO Analysis for Mining**
Our framework covers the factors that matter in mining:
- **People:** Fatigue, training, experience, physical demands
- **Environment:** Ground conditions, ventilation, lighting, dust
- **Equipment:** Mobile equipment, fixed plant, maintenance state
- **Procedures:** SOPs, JSAs, permits, isolation procedures
- **Organization:** Production pressure, staffing, procurement decisions

**3. AI Coaching That Knows Mining**
Our AI suggests questions specific to mining operations:
- "What was the ground condition assessment?"

- "Has this equipment been involved in previous incidents?"

Every finding traces back to decisions:
