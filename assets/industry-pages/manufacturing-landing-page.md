## Section 2: How InvestigatePro Helps Manufacturing

### ICAM Methodology Built for the Plant Floor

**1. Shift-Ready Investigation**
Any supervisor, any shift. Our AI coaching ensures quality investigations whether it's 2 PM or 2 AM.

**2. PEEPO Analysis for Manufacturing**
Our framework covers manufacturing-specific factors:
- **People:** Training, experience, fatigue, ergonomics
- **Environment:** Lighting, noise, housekeeping, layout
- **Equipment:** Guarding, maintenance state, lockout/tagout, ergonomic design
- **Procedures:** SOPs, work instructions, lockout procedures, quality checks
- **Organization:** Production pressure, maintenance scheduling, staffing levels

**3. AI Coaching for Supervisors**
Production supervisors aren't safety professionals. Our AI guides them:
- "Was lockout/tagout verified before the task?"
- "What was the machine's maintenance state?"
- "Were any guards removed or bypassed?"
- "What production pressures existed?"

**4. Equipment Tracking**
Link incidents to specific machines. Identify problem equipment. Build the business case for engineering controls.

**5. Cross-Plant Pattern Recognition**
"Hand injuries across your 3 plants share a common root cause: guard removal for maintenance access."

One insight. Three plants fixed.
