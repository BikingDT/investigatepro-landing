## Section 2: How InvestigatePro Helps Construction

### ICAM Methodology Built for the Jobsite

**1. Mobile-First Investigation**
Supervisors start investigations immediately, on-site. Photos, statements, conditions — captured before evidence disappears or memories fade.

**2. PEEPO Analysis for Construction**
Our framework covers construction-specific factors:
- **People:** Training, experience, language barriers, fatigue
- **Environment:** Weather, site conditions, congestion, access
- **Equipment:** Tools, scaffolding, equipment condition, PPE
- **Procedures:** JSAs, STAs, permits, fall protection plans
- **Organization:** Schedule pressure, subcontractor management, safety investment

**3. AI Coaching for Any Investigator**
Supervisors aren't safety professionals. Our AI guides them:
- "Was a pre-task plan completed?"
- "What was the competent person's assessment?"
- "Were there any recent changes to the work scope?"
- "Has this subcontractor had similar incidents?"

**4. Subcontractor Consistency**
Extend InvestigatePro to your subs. Same methodology, same quality, regardless of who investigates.

**5. Multi-Site Pattern Recognition**
