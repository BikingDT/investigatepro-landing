## General Questions

### What is InvestigatePro?
InvestigatePro is an incident investigation platform built on the ICAM (Incident Cause Analysis Method) methodology. It guides your team through systematic investigations that reach organizational root causes — not just "human error." With AI coaching, any investigator can conduct thorough, consistent investigations that actually prevent repeat incidents.

### Who is InvestigatePro for?
InvestigatePro is designed for safety professionals in high-risk industries:
- **Mining** — Underground, open-pit, processing
- **Construction** — Commercial, industrial, infrastructure
- **Manufacturing** — Automotive, aerospace, heavy industry
- **Oil & Gas** — Upstream, midstream, downstream
- **Utilities** — Power generation, transmission, water

If your organization investigates workplace incidents and wants to prevent them from recurring, InvestigatePro is built for you.

### What is ICAM methodology?
ICAM (Incident Cause Analysis Method) is a systematic approach to incident investigation developed for high-reliability industries. Instead of stopping at "human error," ICAM traces findings through multiple levels — People, Environment, Equipment, Procedures — to reach the Organizational factors where root causes actually live. This approach ensures investigations identify systemic issues that can be fixed, not just individuals to blame.

### How is InvestigatePro different from other safety software?
Most safety software treats incident investigation as one module among many — a form to fill out. InvestigatePro is purpose-built for investigation excellence:

- **Methodology-first:** ICAM is built into every investigation, not optional
- **AI coaching:** Guides any investigator to organizational root causes
- **Pattern recognition:** Identifies trends across incidents automatically
- **Hierarchy of Controls:** Pushes for effective corrective actions, not just "retrain workers"

We do one thing and do it extremely well.

## ICAM & Methodology

### Do I need to be ICAM-trained to use InvestigatePro?
No. InvestigatePro teaches ICAM as you use it. The guided workflow and AI coaching embed methodology into every investigation.

That said, ICAM training is valuable context. We offer training resources and can recommend certification programs.

### What's the difference between ICAM and other methodologies (5 Whys, Fishbone, etc.)?
**5 Whys:** Simple technique to drill down to root causes. Useful but unstructured.

**Fishbone (Ishikawa):** Visual tool to categorize potential causes. Good for brainstorming.

**ICAM:** Comprehensive methodology that systematically traces findings through multiple levels to organizational factors. More rigorous and consistent.

InvestigatePro incorporates 5 Whys within the ICAM framework. You get the benefits of both.

### What is PEEPO?
PEEPO is the analysis framework within ICAM:
- **P**eople — Human factors (fatigue, training, experience)
- **E**nvironment — Physical conditions (lighting, layout, weather)
- **E**quipment — Tools and machinery (design, maintenance, guarding)
- **P**rocedures — Documented vs actual work methods
- **O**rganization — Decisions that created the above conditions

Every factor in P-E-E-P traces back to an Organizational factor. That's where root causes live.

### How does InvestigatePro handle "human error" findings?
We don't accept "human error" as a root cause. When investigators identify human factors, InvestigatePro prompts them to dig deeper:

- "The worker rushed. What created the time pressure?"
- "The procedure wasn't followed. Why was it impractical?"
- "Training was inadequate. What limited the training investment?"

Human error is where lazy investigations stop. With InvestigatePro, it's where thorough investigations start.
