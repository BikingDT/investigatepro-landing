## FAQ Section

### How does billing work?
Plans are billed monthly or annually. Annual billing saves 20% (2 months free).

### Can I change plans later?
Yes. Upgrade anytime (immediate). Downgrade at next billing cycle.

### What counts as a "user"?
Anyone who logs into InvestigatePro to conduct or review investigations. Read-only dashboard viewers don't count against your limit.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees. Ever.

### Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?
Yes. Contact us for nonprofit pricing.

### What happens if I exceed my user limit?
We'll notify you and help you upgrade. We don't cut off access mid-investigation.

### Can I try before I buy?
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.
