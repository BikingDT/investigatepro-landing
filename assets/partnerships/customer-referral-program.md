## FAQ

### Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?
No limit. Refer as many as you want.

### When do I get my credit?
After the referred person completes their first paid month (not during free trial).

### Can I refer someone from my own company?
If they're setting up a separate account, yes. If you're adding users to your existing account, that's not a referral.

### What if they cancel?
If they cancel within 30 days, the credit is reversed. After 30 days, you keep it.

### Can I combine with other offers?
Yes, referral discounts stack with founding customer offers and other promotions.
