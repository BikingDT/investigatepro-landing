The Problem with 5 Whys Today

5 Whys is powerful — when done right. But most investigators struggle.

Stop Too Soon

Most people stop at 2-3 whys, missing the systemic root causes that actually prevent recurrence.

No Challenge

Without a skilled facilitator to challenge assumptions, investigators accept weak answers.

Inconsistent Quality

Results vary wildly depending on who's doing the analysis. No standardized approach across the team.

How 5 Whys AI Works

An AI coach that guides you through rigorous root cause analysis — in the field or at your desk.

1

Describe the Problem

Enter what happened — text, voice, or photos. The clearer the problem statement, the better the analysis.

2

Answer the Whys

For each "Why," the AI asks follow-up questions and challenges weak answers to push you deeper.

3

Explore Branches

Real incidents have multiple causes. Branch your analysis to capture the full causal picture.

4

Export & Integrate

Share your analysis as PDF, or sync directly to InvestigatePro for a complete investigation record.
