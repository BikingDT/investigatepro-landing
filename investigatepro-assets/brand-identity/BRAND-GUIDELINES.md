# InvestigatePro Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**InvestigatePro** is a professional incident investigation SaaS platform built for safety professionals. Our brand conveys trust, expertise, and modern technology while maintaining approachability for industrial environments.

### Brand Personality
- **Professional** — Trusted by safety leaders in high-stakes industries
- **Intelligent** — AI-powered insights without complexity
- **Approachable** — Enterprise capability, startup simplicity
- **Modern** — Contemporary design that stands out from legacy software

---

## Logo

### Primary Logo
The InvestigatePro logo consists of two elements:
1. **The IP Monogram** — A geometric lettermark combining "I" and "P"
2. **The Wordmark** — "InvestigatePro" in clean sans-serif typography

### Logo Versions

| Version | File | Use Case |
|---------|------|----------|
| Primary (Dark BG) | `logo-03-on-dark.png` | Dark backgrounds, presentations, hero sections |
| Primary (Light BG) | `logo-03-lettermark-ip.png` | White/light backgrounds, documents |
| Icon Only | `logo-03-icon-only.png` | App icons, favicons, social avatars |
| Horizontal | `logo-03-horizontal.png` | Website headers, email signatures |
| Monochrome | `logo-03-mono-black.png` | Single-color printing, watermarks |

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "I" in the monogram.

### Minimum Size
- **Digital:** 32px height minimum for icon, 120px for full logo
- **Print:** 0.5" height minimum for icon, 1.5" for full logo

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort
- ❌ Don't change colors outside brand palette
- ❌ Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- ❌ Don't rotate or flip

## Iconography

### Style Guidelines
- **Stroke weight:** 1.5px - 2px
- **Corner radius:** Rounded (2-4px)
- **Style:** Outlined preferred, filled for emphasis
- **Size:** 24px default, scale in 8px increments

### Recommended Icon Libraries
1. **Lucide** (Primary) — https://lucide.dev
2. **Heroicons** (Alternative) — https://heroicons.com

## UI Components

### Buttons

#### Primary Button
- Background: `#76DCEC` (Cyan 500)
- Text: `#0C142E` (Navy 900)
- Border Radius: 8px
- Padding: 12px 24px
- Hover: `#88E0EE` (Cyan 400)

- Background: Transparent
- Border: 1px solid `#76DCEC`
- Text: `#76DCEC`

- Background: `#FFFFFF` or `#1A2744` (on dark)
- Border Radius: 12px
- Shadow: `0 4px 6px -1px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1)`
- Padding: 24px

- Input Background: `#F8FAFC`
- Border: 1px solid `#CBD5E1`
- Focus Border: `#76DCEC`
- Border Radius: 8px

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Clear:** No jargon, explain complex concepts simply
- **Confident:** Expert knowledge without arrogance
- **Supportive:** Helping users succeed, not lecturing
- **Action-oriented:** Focus on what users can do

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and scannable
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Address the reader directly ("you")
