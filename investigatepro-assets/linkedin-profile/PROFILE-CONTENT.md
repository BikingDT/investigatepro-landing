## About Section

```
After 15+ years investigating incidents in mining and high-risk industries, I kept seeing the same problems:

→ Investigations that drag on for weeks
→ Critical data missed because there's no structured approach
→ Root causes that never get to the real "why"

I've led safety teams, conducted hundreds of investigations, and seen what works. More importantly, I've seen what doesn't.
```
