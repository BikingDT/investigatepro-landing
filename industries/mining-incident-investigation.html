The Problem

Why Mining Incident Investigations Often Fall Short

Mining has unique hazards, complex multi-contractor sites, and regulators who increasingly expect systemic analysis. Generic investigation tools — or paper forms — aren't up to the job.

📋

Paper Forms That Miss Causal Factors

Static Word documents and paper-based investigation forms don't prompt investigators to look beyond immediate causes. Organizational factors go unrecorded. The same incidents recur.

🏗️

Multi-Contractor Site Complexity

Modern mines operate with dozens of contractors. When incidents involve multiple companies, investigation accountability becomes unclear and findings don't flow back to all affected parties.

📊

No Cross-Site Learning

Valuable lessons from one site never reach other operations. Investigations disappear into shared drives. Trends go undetected until a pattern of serious incidents demands executive attention.

⚖️

Regulatory Pressure Growing

Mining regulators in Australia, South Africa, West Africa, and the Middle East increasingly require ICAM-aligned investigations for serious incidents. Informal processes no longer satisfy compliance requirements.

🕐

Investigations That Take Too Long

When investigators must build everything from scratch — timeline, causal analysis, PEEPO assessment, recommendations — a simple ICAM investigation can take days. The process becomes a burden.

🔁

Recommendations That Aren't Implemented

Investigation reports generate corrective actions that sit in email inboxes. Without a tracking system, overdue actions go unescalated. The same root causes resurface in the next incident.

Industry Coverage

Built for Mining's Specific Hazards

InvestigatePro's investigation framework and AI coaching are calibrated for the incident types most common in mining operations.

🪨

Ground Control & Rockfall

Underground stope failures, hangingwall instability, rockburst events. ICAM analysis surfaces geotechnical management system gaps, inspection frequency, and support design processes.

🚛

Mobile Equipment & Haul Roads

Collision, rollover, and pedestrian interaction incidents. Investigation covers traffic management systems, equipment inspection regimes, fatigue management, and site layout design.

💥

Blasting & Explosives

Misfires, fly rock, premature detonation. ICAM captures deviations from shot firing procedures, competency verification systems, and initiation system management.

⚗️

Processing Plant Incidents

Mechanical failures, chemical exposures, conveyor accidents. Covers maintenance management systems, LOTO/LOTOTO procedures, permit-to-work, and change management.

🫁

Occupational Health & Airborne Hazards

Dust and silica exposure, vibration, heat stress incidents. Investigation examines monitoring program design, health surveillance systems, and occupational hygiene management.

⚡

Electrical & High-Energy Hazards

Electrical contact, arc flash, high-pressure systems. ICAM analysis covers isolation management systems, energy control procedures, and competency verification for high-risk work.

🚧

Falls from Height & Slips/Trips

Working at height incidents including ladderways, platforms, and dump edges. Examines fall protection systems, inspection regimes, and task-specific risk assessment quality.

🏗️

Contractor & Supply Chain Management

Incidents involving contractor personnel. ICAM captures contractor management systems, pre-qualification standards, site induction quality, and principal-contractor interface management.

How It Works

An Investigation in 5 Steps

From incident notification to closed corrective actions — InvestigatePro guides your team through every step.

1

Log the Incident

Record incident details, classification (LTI, HIPO, near-miss), location, personnel involved, and initial description. Takes 2 minutes. Can be done on mobile from the mine site.

2

Build the Timeline

Reconstruct the sequence of events using the guided timeline builder. Attach photos, witness statements, and evidence documents. AI flags gaps in the sequence.

3

ICAM Causal Analysis

Work through the four ICAM factor categories: absent/failed defences, individual/team actions, task/environmental conditions, and PEEPO organizational factors. AI coaching ensures depth and rigor.

4

Generate the Report

One click generates a complete, formatted investigation report. Includes all evidence, timeline, causal analysis, and recommendations. Regulator-ready immediately.

5

Track Corrective Actions to Closure

All recommendations are tracked as actions. Automated reminders notify owners before due dates. Overdue actions escalate to supervisors. Investigations don't close until actions are complete and verified.
