The Challenge

Why O&G Incident Investigations Demand More

High-energy systems, complex process interfaces, remote operations, and strict regulatory frameworks make incident investigation in oil and gas uniquely demanding.

⚙️

Process Safety Complexity

O&G incidents often involve multiple simultaneous barrier failures. A surface-level investigation that identifies one immediate cause misses the systemic conditions that made the incident possible.

🌍

Remote & Offshore Operations

Incidents on remote wellsites, offshore platforms, or pipeline corridors require investigators to work without immediate access to specialists, documentation systems, or laboratory support.

📜

Intensive Regulatory Scrutiny

OSHA PSM, EPA RMP, API standards, and host-country regulations impose detailed investigation requirements. Reports must demonstrate systematic causal analysis and documented corrective actions.

🤝

Contractor Workforce Dominance

Many O&G operations run with 70–90% contractor workforce. When contractor personnel are injured, investigation ownership, data access, and corrective action accountability become genuinely difficult.

⏰

24/7 Operational Pressure

Production targets, drilling schedules, and pipeline commitments create constant pressure to resume operations quickly after an incident. Investigations get cut short. Root causes go unaddressed.

📊

Isolated Investigation Silos

Findings from one asset rarely reach other operations. The same process safety vulnerabilities — management of change gaps, barrier degradation, permit failures — surface repeatedly across facilities.

Hazard Coverage

Built for O&G's Hazard Landscape

InvestigatePro's ICAM framework addresses the incident types most critical to oil, gas, and petrochemical operations.

💥

Well Control & Blowout Events

Kick detection failures, BOP malfunction, pressure control incidents. ICAM surfaces well program design quality, competency verification systems, and real-time monitoring processes.

🔥

Fire, Explosion & Hydrocarbon Release

Process containment failures, ignition events, and uncontrolled releases. Investigation examines mechanical integrity programs, management of change systems, and hot work permit quality.

⚗️

Chemical & Toxic Exposure

H₂S exposure, chemical spills, inhalation incidents. ICAM captures atmospheric monitoring programs, emergency response system design, personal protective equipment selection, and muster systems.

🔧

Isolation & Maintenance Incidents

LOTO/LOTOTO failures, pressure isolation incidents, and unexpected energy releases during maintenance. Examines isolation management systems, permit-to-work quality, and verification procedures.

🚁

Lifting & Rigging Operations

Dropped objects, crane failures, and rigging incidents on platform decks and rig floors. Investigation covers lift plan quality, equipment inspection regimes, and banksman competency systems.

🌊

Marine & Diving Operations

Vessel collision, man-overboard, dive incidents. ICAM captures pre-job risk assessment quality, communication systems, environmental monitoring, and emergency response preparedness.

🛣️

Road Transport & Vehicle Safety

One of the leading causes of fatality in O&G globally. ICAM investigation of road incidents captures journey management systems, fatigue management, vehicle condition monitoring, and driver competency.

🏚️

Camp & Accommodation Incidents

Food safety, fire, and personal safety incidents in remote accommodation. Investigates facility management systems, contractor camp standards, and occupational health monitoring.
