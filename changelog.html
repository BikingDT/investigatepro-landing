🚀 Official Launch

InvestigatePro is now publicly available! AI-powered incident investigation software built on real ICAM methodology.

✨ Core Features

  • Full ICAM methodology framework with guided workflows
  • AI Investigation Coach with real-time suggestions
  • 5-Whys analysis wizard with AI-powered recommendations
  • PEEPO (People, Environment, Equipment, Procedures, Organization) analysis
  • Executive dashboard with pSIF focus and real-time KPIs
  • Investigation pipeline tracking from report to completion
  • Corrective action management with due date tracking
  • Multi-site support with location-based risk visualization

📊 Reporting & Analytics

  • Automated compliance reports
  • Trend analysis across incidents
  • Severity and root cause breakdowns
  • Export to PDF and Excel

🔐 Security & Compliance

  • Full audit logging
  • GDPR compliance with data export/delete
  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
