üìã ICAM Methodology

What is ICAM? ‚ñº
ICAM (Incident Cause Analysis Method) is a systematic approach to investigating workplace incidents. It examines four key areas: Absent/Failed Defenses, Individual/Team Actions, Task/Environmental Conditions, and Organisational Factors. This helps identify not just what happened, but why it happened and how to prevent recurrence.
Do I need ICAM training to use InvestigatePro? ‚ñº
No! Our AI coaching guides you through each step of the ICAM process. It's like having an expert investigation coach available 24/7. The platform explains each concept as you go and suggests what to analyze next.
What's the difference between ICAM and other investigation methods? ‚ñº
Unlike simple "blame the worker" approaches, ICAM looks at systemic factors. It examines organizational issues, management systems, and workplace conditions ‚Äî not just individual actions. This leads to more effective corrective actions that actually prevent future incidents.
