What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how the site is being used.

How to Manage Cookies

Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy and Security ‚Üí Cookies
  • Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Cookies
  • Edge: Settings ‚Üí Privacy, Search, and Services ‚Üí Cookies

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

  • You may not be able to log in to your account
  • Some features may not work correctly
  • We won't be able to remember your preferences
