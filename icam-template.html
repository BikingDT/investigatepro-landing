Section 1: Initial Response

Immediate Actions (First 60 Minutes)

  • Ensure scene is safe for investigators
  • Confirm injured persons are receiving care
  • Secure the incident scene (barriers, signage)
  • Identify and preserve evidence
  • Identify witnesses
  • Begin preliminary documentation (photos, notes)
  • Notify required parties (management, regulators)
