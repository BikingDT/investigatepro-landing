Best for: Serious incidents in high-hazard industries (mining, oil & gas, construction). Based on the Incident Cause Analysis Method — the gold standard for complex, multi-causal events.

Incident Classification

ICAM Analysis — Four Factor Categories

ICAM structures causes across four levels. Work upwards from immediate events to organisational factors.

1. Absent / Failed Defences — What barriers were missing or failed?
2. Individual / Team Actions — What did people do or not do?
3. Task / Environmental Conditions — What workplace conditions contributed?
4. Organisational Factors — What management system or cultural issues enabled this?
