Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just another audit tool?

No. SafetyCulture and similar tools are great for inspections and audits. InvestigatePro is specifically for incident investigation — finding root causes and preventing recurrence.

Do we need training to use it?

The interface is intuitive, but more importantly, the system guides you through proper investigation methodology. The AI coaching helps even inexperienced investigators ask the right questions.

What's pSIF classification?

pSIF (potential Serious Injury & Fatality) is a methodology for identifying incidents that could have resulted in death or permanent disability. Our system automatically flags these based on industry-standard criteria.

Can we import existing incidents?

Yes. We offer CSV import and can help migrate your historical data during onboarding.

Is our data secure?

Yes. SOC 2 compliant, encrypted at rest and in transit, hosted on AWS. Your data is never shared or used for training.
